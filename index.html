About

Welcome to Tailored Technology Ltd established since 1989. Led by Mahendra Tailor, a Chartered Engineer specializing in embedded systems and wireless communications. With decades of experience, Mahendra was one of the first to invent a Bluetooth module, driving innovation in wireless technology. His expertise spans Bluetooth, UWB, LE Audio, and advanced firmware development for low-power wireless modules.
